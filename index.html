About me

I am a third year graduate student working with Elena D'Onghia at the University of Wisconsin - Madison.

My work focuses on the asymmetries in Magellanic Irregular galaxies - galaxies that, like the Large Magellanic Cloud, have misplaced bars, single spiral arms, or irregular features.

We use a custom version of the GalIC code to simulate isolated galaxies and evolve encounters with Gadget 3.
